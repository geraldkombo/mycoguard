# Judges Demo Script (3 Minutes)

**Minute 0:00 – 1:00: The Zero-Connectivity Promise**
"Distinguished judges, welcome to MycoGuard Kenya. Aflatoxin prevention fails when the internet fails. I am now putting this device into Airplane Mode." *(Demonstrate turning on Airplane Mode.)* "Watch as MycoGuard loads instantly. We are targeting the '10 Places' where contamination thrives — from the Field to the Milk Context. Let's enter 'Group Facilitator Mode', designed for a youth extension worker assessing 15 farmers today."

**Minute 1:00 – 2:00: The 3-Layer Weather System (Offline Alerts)**
"MycoGuard doesn't depend on live weather APIs or SMS. Instead, it gives farmers a powerful offline alert workflow. I tap 'Today's Conditions' and select 'Raining / Flooding'. Instantly, a red banner appears with the top 3 critical actions to save the grain from re-wetting and ensure it reaches the safe 13.5% moisture threshold. Next, I select 'Add reminders to phone'. The app generates a calendar file — an `.ics` download — that sets up native phone notifications for evening tarp coverage and weekly storage checks. It acts like a push notification, but it's 100% offline and free."

**Minute 2:00 – 3:00: Verified Directory & Evidence Export**
"If the crop is too wet, traditional sun-drying fails. I open the 'Verified Drying Directory'. This is not crowdsourced guesswork. Here is the NCPB commercial drying service, marked 'Official' with verified pricing of Ksh 70 per 90kg bag. Finally, we must document our One Health impact. I click 'Export Evidence'. The app generates a clean PDF report and a CSV data backup. All offline. All free. Thank you."
