# One Health Impact & Proxy Indicators

*Disclaimer: MycoGuard Kenya is a risk-screening and behavioral intervention tool. It does NOT diagnose aflatoxin levels. Proxies are used to measure risk reduction.*

## Process Indicators (Adoption & Reach)
1. **Assessments Completed:** number of risk checklists completed per county (captured via exported CSVs from facilitators).
2. **Calendar Reminders Set:** number of `.ics` weather risk files generated and downloaded.
3. **Directory Engagement:** number of times the "Verified Drying Directory" is accessed for routing grain.
4. **Group Sprints Executed:** number of group sessions logged via Group Facilitator Mode.

## Outcome Proxy Indicators (Behavioral Shifts)
1. **Drying Practice Shift:** % increase in farmers reporting use of tarpaulins/canvas instead of bare soil (Field/Harvest → Drying transition).
2. **Storage Improvement:** % increase in use of pallets/dunnage to keep bags off the floor (Home Store/Aggregation transition).
3. **Safe Feed Diversion Rate:** reduction in the reported practice of feeding visibly moldy or downgraded grains to lactating dairy cows (interrupting the AFB1 → AFM1 pathway).
4. **Safe Removal:** % of users acknowledging the "remove from food/feed chain and follow county health guidance" protocol.
