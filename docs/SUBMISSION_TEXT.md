# 3rd Kenya National Agroecology Symposium Submission

**Innovation Title:** MycoGuard Kenya: Offline One Health Risk-to-Action Kit
**Innovator(s):** Gerald Kombo
**Category:** Youth / Institution [select one]
**Justification:** Equipping frontline extension workers, youth facilitators, and farmer field schools with zero-cost, offline risk intelligence to secure community food and feed systems.

### Problem Statement (145 words)
Kenya's food safety and dairy sectors face overlapping crises driven by climate extremes. Drought and heat stress weaken crop defenses, while erratic heavy rains and flooding cause post-harvest re-wetting, creating perfect conditions for *Aspergillus* fungi. Contaminated maize is frequently diverted to livestock, transforming Aflatoxin B1 into carcinogenic Aflatoxin M1 in milk, contaminating food supplies and contributing to childhood stunting. In June 2026, KALRO testing operations detected alarming levels of aflatoxin contamination in cereal products, with some samples containing up to 500 ppb — fifty times the legal limit. However, prohibitive laboratory testing costs keep ordinary farmers in the dark. A scalable, offline solution is urgently needed to translate local weather risks and handling practices into actionable, preventative One Health interventions at the grassroots level without relying on expensive testing.

### Innovation Description (298 words)
MycoGuard Kenya is an offline-first Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to intercept aflatoxin risks across the agricultural value chain. Moving beyond costly lab dependency, it provides verifiable risk screening and prevention guidance.

**Strategic Alignment:**
MycoGuard aligns with the FAO 10 Elements of Agroecology (promoting Resilience and Knowledge Co-creation) and the Quadripartite One Health Joint Plan of Action. By securing feed and food, it disrupts the dangerous feed-to-milk pathway (AFB1 to AFM1).

**Core Features & Scalability:**
- **10 Places Pathway:** tracks risk across 10 distinct vulnerability points — Field → Harvest → Drying → Shelling/Milling → Home store → Transport → Trader/Market → Aggregation → Feed store → Milk context.
- **No-Backend Offline-First:** entirely local-storage based (IndexedDB). No live APIs, SMS, or cloud sync — 100% functional in deep rural areas. Exports JSON, CSV, and PDF reports directly from the device.
- **Group Facilitator Mode:** built for Farmer Field Schools and market sprints, enabling rapid bulk assessments.
- **3-Layer Weather Risk System:** since live push notifications require internet, MycoGuard uses an offline alternative — observed-condition alerts, seasonal preparedness guidance, and `.ics` calendar exports for native phone reminders.
- **Verified Drying Directory:** an offline database of confirmed grain drying services (e.g. GDC Menengai, NCPB) with cited URLs to prevent misinformation.

**International Days Activation Plan:**
A localized toolkit for World Food Safety Day (7 June), World Food Day (16 October), World One Health Day (3 November), and World AMR Awareness Week (18–24 November).

**June 2026 Market Drying & Storage Sprint:**
In response to erratic MAM rainfall patterns, MycoGuard features a dedicated deployment sprint targeting aggregators and dairy cooperatives, offering immediate triage for waterlogged grain and promoting verified mechanical drying access.
